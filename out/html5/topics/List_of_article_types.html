List of article types

Follow this reference to determine what to write in the article summary based on its article type.

Article type What to include in the Article Summary
Incident Models List the application technologies.
Unit Overviews List the group(s) which support the department.
Published Articles (articles that will be public-facing) Copy the first sentence of the article.
Other article types Include a short statement that clarifies the title, if necessary.
